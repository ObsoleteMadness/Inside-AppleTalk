    AppleTalk Documentation#

    Welcome to the community-maintained AppleTalk documentation site. This project takes original Apple technical documents, OCRs them, and publishes them here in an easy-to-read, searchable format.

    What Is AppleTalk?#

    AppleTalk is the suite of proprietary networking protocols developed by Apple Computer for their Macintosh computers and peripherals. First introduced in 1985 alongside the original LaserWriter, AppleTalk provided plug-and-play networking years before such concepts became mainstream.
